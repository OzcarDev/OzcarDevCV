About the Project

Explore the fascinating history of Mexican innovation with our exciting game, Timeline of Mexican Inventors!

Embark on an interactive journey through time and discover the brilliant minds who have left an indelible mark on the world of invention and creativity.

In this captivating interactive timeline, you'll enjoy an educational adventure full of discoveries while learning about Mexican inventors who have revolutionized fields ranging from technology to medicine and beyond.

Platform: Web Browser
Engine: Unity
Development Time: 4 months
Team Size: 5 developers

Key Features

  • Explore a visually engaging timeline showcasing the most influential Mexican inventors
  • Educational Gameplay
  • Unlock detailed profiles and stories of inventors as you progress through the timeline
