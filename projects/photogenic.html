About the Project

Photogenic is a first-person 3D game where players explore a vibrant and quirky city, searching for the best angles and compositions to capture through their camera lens.

An unemployed and bankrupt photographer is desperately trying to get his job back. To prove his worth to his boss, he starts a new project: filling a photo album with the most memorable and breathtaking moments of his city.

Will you help him achieve his goal?

Platform: Windows PC
Engine: Unity
Development Time: 6 months
Team Size: 1 developers

Key Features

  • Dense and Alive City to Explore
  • Inspect objects around the city to learn more about its stories and secrets.
  • Fully Functional Camera System
  • Complete your photography list by finding and capturing all required subjects across the city.
  • Retro 3D Pixel-Art Aesthetic
  • Relaxing Soundtrack
