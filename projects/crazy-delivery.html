About the Project

Deliver as many pizzas as you can in a city full of chaos, explosions, and pure fun! Dodge obstacles, break the laws of physics, and become the ultimate master of extreme driving.

Collect unique vehicles, each with its own style and handling. Deliver pizzas in record time to earn coins and unlock even crazier rides.

Show off your skills in Crazy Delivery! Discover secret routes, challenge your friends, and become part of the legend of the wildest, most explosive pizza delivery ever. Ready for the madness on wheels?

Platform: Android
Engine: Unity
Development Time: 6 months
Team Size: 2 developers

Key Features

  • Fast-Paced Pizza Delivery Chaos
  • Open Map with Multiple Routes
  • Unlockable & Unique Vehicles
  • Dynamic VFX & Interactions
  • Arcade Progression System
  • 1k+ downloads and a 4.8/5 rating from real players.
